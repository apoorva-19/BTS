Irma aims at showcasing the talent of the local craftsmen and provides a window to have an inside peek into the making of these bamboo products. Witness and discover the creative process and learn about the hidden stories behind each piece.

Aim

The idea is to expand the demand and the opportunity of bamboo artists and enhance the ability to learn the craft.

Target Audience

The exhibition to caters to learners who could be students or professional artists and buyers such as art collectors and designers.
