An exhibition that characterises each sign through tangible aspect of space. Giving a broader prespective on a topic that is less dealt with in the exhibition and museum space.

Aim

A space where each one can recognise a part of themselves. Create self reflection and retrospection within each visitor by sparking the innate curiosity that each person has with their own nature.

Target Audience

The nature of the topic allows the exhibition to cater to a wide demographic of audience. It targets the curiosity within each person to find out more about themselves from the world around them.
